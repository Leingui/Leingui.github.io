    • Shawn Mendes

    Shawn Mendes was born in Toronto, Canada on August 8th of 1998. He is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and model. He gained fame by posting 6-second videos when he was 14 years old in an application known as Vine. With Vine, he caught the attention of Andrew Gentler, and American talent manager. After a couple of years, he released his first single called “Life of the Party,“ which made him the youngest artist with a debut single in the Top 25. Life of the Party

    His music became so famous that he received two Grammy award nominations.
